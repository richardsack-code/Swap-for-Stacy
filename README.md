My best friend, Max, had a BMW 3-series. Her name was Stacy.  
Unfortunately, Stacy's gear box broke in the middle of the highway, from which I had to pick Max up.  
When he had Stacy scrapped he received the generous amount of one single Euro, which he gave to me, 
together with the task of coming up with something cool to do with that euro.

Have you heard of the guy, that traded a red paperclip for a house? Well, not with a single trade, but with 14.
Read all about it here: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/One_red_paperclip  
Anyway, this is me hopefully recreating that insane story, but starting with a euro instead.  
I hope, to amuse people with the idea and that they will trade for cool stuff.  
I am not quite sure, what the end-goal is yet, but I know it's going to be awesome!
